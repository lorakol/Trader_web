Read MoreOrderbook Walls are very large Limit Orders placed in the Orderbook for a Market

Orderbook Walls are able to show where people who have very large positions in place and may give some indication of the market's sentiment. Although Orderbooks can be false by being placed and withdrawn quickly, the Walls below are shown with their Age and how much they have changed since they were first detected. Walls that have an Age of days and even weeks, can represent a large interest at specific Prices that may be considered a reduction in a "false reading". These Walls are in the Bitmex "Futures Markets" and although Walls with an Age may be false, the chance of being liquidated with your position in Bitmex reduces the "false readings" simply by how large these Walls are that the risk of millions of dollars is real. By observing the walls and viewing them on the Graph, you may create a Strategy and Algorithm based on the Wall Data. You can use Walls to place Stop Losses behind so if these Walls are taken by the Market, then you should Stop Loss at that point as they are large and indicating the market moving. You can also use the Walls to place Take Profits before them, so in case the price does reach these Walls and stop, your Take Profit order will be taken and not miss out on a price surge that did not make it through the Wall.
